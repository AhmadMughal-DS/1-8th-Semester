# Page 8: Verb Avoir (Paas Hona / Rakhna)

---

## Table Format

| **English** | **French** | **English Pronunciation** | **Urdu Pronunciation** |
|-------------|-----------|---------------------------|--------------------------|
| I have | J'ai | zhay | ژے |
| You have (informal) | Tu as | too ah | تو آ |
| He/She has | Il/Elle a | eel/el ah | ایل آ |
| We have | Nous avons | noo zah-von | نو زاوون |
| You have (formal/plural) | Vous avez | voo zah-vay | وو زاوے |
| They have | Ils/Elles ont | eel/el on | ایل اون |

---

## Word by Word Explanation (Roman Urdu Mein)

### I have = J'ai (ژے)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "zhay"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Zhay  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Mere paas hai". Asal mein "Je ai" tha, ab "J'ai" ho gaya.  
**Misaal:** "J'ai un livre" (Mere paas ek kitaab hai)

---

### You have (informal) = Tu as (تو آ)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "too ah"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Too ah  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Tumhare paas hai". Chhota sa.  
**Misaal:** "Tu as un chat" (Tumhare paas ek billi hai)

---

### He/She has = Il/Elle a (ایل آ)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "eel/el ah"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Eel/El ah  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Uske paas hai". Bas "ah" ki awaaz.  
**Misaal:** "Il a une voiture" (Uske paas ek gaari hai)

---

### We have = Nous avons (نو زاوون)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "noo zah-von"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Noo zah-von  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Hamare paas hai". "Avons" mein 'v' ki awaaz.  
**Misaal:** "Nous avons une maison" (Hamare paas ek ghar hai)

---

### You have (formal/plural) = Vous avez (وو زاوے)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "voo zah-vay"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Voo zah-vay  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Aapke paas hai" ya "Tum sab ke paas hai".  
**Misaal:** "Vous avez raison" (Aap sahi hain - idiom)

---

### They have = Ils/Elles ont (ایل اون)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "eel/el on"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Eel/El on  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Unke paas hai". Nasal se "on" bolo.  
**Misaal:** "Ils ont des amis" (Unke dost hain)

---

## Verb "Avoir" Ka Poora Chart

```
J'ai        (mere paas hai)

Il a        (uske paas hai - larka)
Elle a      (uske paas hai - larki)
Nous avons  (hamare paas hai)
Vous avez   (aapke paas hai)
Ils ont     (unke paas hai - larke)
Elles ont   (unke paas hai - larkiyan)
```

---

## Special Uses (Khaas Istemal)

### Umar Batana

**French mein umar batane ke liye "avoir" istemal hota hai, "être" nahi!**

- J'ai 15 ans (Meri umar 15 saal hai) ❌ NOT "Je suis 15 ans"
- Tu as 20 ans (Tumhari umar 20 saal hai)
- Il a 10 ans (Uski umar 10 saal hai)

### Feelings/Conditions

- J'ai faim (Mujhe bhook lagi hai)
- J'ai soif (Mujhe pyaas lagi hai)
- J'ai froid (Mujhe thand lag rahi hai)
- J'ai chaud (Mujhe garmi lag rahi hai)

---

## Quick Summary

**Pattern:**
- J'ai, Tu as, Il/Elle a - short forms
- Nous avons, Vous avez - "av-" se shuru
- Ils/Elles ont - "on" se khatam

**Yaad Rakhein:**
- Umar = "avoir" use karo (J'ai 15 ans)
- Bhook/Pyaas = "avoir" use karo (J'ai faim/soif)
- Yeh "être" ke baad doosra sabse zaroori verb hai!

# Verb Aller (Jana)

---

## Table Format

| **English** | **French** | **English Pronunciation** | **Urdu Pronunciation** |
|-------------|-----------|---------------------------|--------------------------|
| I go | Je vais | zhuh vay | ژہ وے |
| You go (informal) | Tu vas | too vah | تو وا |
| He/She goes | Il/Elle va | eel/el vah | ایل وا |
| We go | Nous allons | noo zah-lon | نو زالون |
| You go (formal/plural) | Vous allez | voo zah-lay | وو زالے |
| They go | Ils/Elles vont | eel/el von | ایل وون |

---

## Word by Word Explanation (Roman Urdu Mein)

### I go = Je vais (ژہ وے)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "zhuh vay"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Zhuh vay  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Main jata/jati hoon". "Vais" ka matlab hai "jata hoon".  
**Misaal:** "Je vais à l'école" (Main school jata hoon)

---

### You go (informal) = Tu vas (تو وا)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "too vah"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Too vah  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Tum jate/jati ho". Dost ko bolo.  
**Misaal:** "Tu vas au marché" (Tum bazar jate ho)

---

### He/She goes = Il/Elle va (ایل وا)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "eel/el vah"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Eel/El vah  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Woh jata/jati hai". Sabse chhota form.  
**Misaal:** "Elle va au cinéma" (Woh cinema jati hai)

---

### We go = Nous allons (نو زالون)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "noo zah-lon"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Noo zah-lon  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Hum jate hain". "Allons" bilkul alag dikhta hai.  
**Misaal:** "Nous allons à Paris" (Hum Paris jate hain)

---

### You go (formal/plural) = Vous allez (وو زالے)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "voo zah-lay"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Voo zah-lay  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Aap jate hain" ya "Tum sab jate ho".  
**Misaal:** "Vous allez bien?" (Aap kaise hain? - idiom)

---

### They go = Ils/Elles vont (ایل وون)

**Bolne ka tareeqa:** "eel/el von"  
**Roman Urdu mein:** Eel/El von  
**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa:** "Woh (sab) jate hain". Nasal se "von" bolo.  
**Misaal:** "Ils vont à la plage" (Woh beach par jate hain)

---

## Verb "Aller" Ka Poora Chart

```
Je vais      (main jata/jati hoon)

Il va        (woh jata hai - larka)
Elle va      (woh jati hai - larki)
Nous allons  (hum jate hain)
Vous allez   (aap jate hain)
Ils vont     (woh jate hain - larke)
Elles vont   (woh jati hain - larkiyan)
```

---

## Special Uses (Khaas Istemal)

### Future Tense Banana (Near Future)

**"Aller" + infinitive verb = kuch karne wala/wali hoon**

- Je vais manger (Main khane wala hoon)
- Tu vas étudier (Tum parhne wale ho)
- Il va jouer (Woh khelne wala hai)
- Nous allons partir (Hum jane wale hain)

### Haal-e-Sehat Poochna

- Comment allez-vous? (Aap kaise hain? - formal)
- Comment vas-tu? (Tum kaise ho? - informal)
- Ça va? (Kaisa hai? - casual)

### Common Expressions

- Aller à pied (Paidal jana)
- Aller en voiture (Gaari se jana)
- Aller en train (Train se jana)
- Allons-y! (Chalo! / Let's go!)

---

## Prepositions with Places (Jagah ke saath)

### "À" (to) - Cities & Specific Places
- Je vais **à** Paris (Main Paris ja raha hoon)
- Tu vas **à** l'école (Tum school ja rahe ho)
- Il va **à** la maison (Woh ghar ja raha hai)

### "En" (to) - Feminine Countries
- Nous allons **en** France (Hum France ja rahe hain)
- Vous allez **en** Italie (Aap Italy ja rahe hain)

### "Au" (to) - Masculine Countries
- Ils vont **au** Pakistan (Woh Pakistan ja rahe hain)
- Je vais **au** Canada (Main Canada ja raha hoon)

---

## Quick Summary

**Pattern:**
- Je vais, Tu vas, Il/Elle va - "va-" se shuru
- Nous allons, Vous allez - "all-" se shuru (bilkul different!)
- Ils/Elles vont - "von" (nasal sound)

**Yaad Rakhein:**
- "Aller" ek irregular verb hai (rules follow nahi karta)
- Future batane ke liye: aller + verb (Je vais manger)
- Haal poochne ke liye: "Comment allez-vous?"
- Yeh teen sabse zaroori verbs mein se ek hai (être, avoir, aller)!
